About Paw & Clover

A cozy little shop for pets and their people

Paw & Clover was designed as a warm, modern pet store concept for a portfolio project. The goal was to create a believable small-business website with a welcoming brand identity, featured products, service options, and clear sections that feel useful to real customers.

This concept could easily be customized for a local pet boutique, groomer, or specialty animal supply shop.
